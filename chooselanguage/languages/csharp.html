C#


Language Classification:

  • Difficulty: Intermediate

  • Front / Back-End: Back-End

  • High / Low Level: High

  • Interpreted / Compiled: Compiled

What do these mean?

C# is a popular language created by Microsoft. It is a very fast language, and is used in video games, as well as the backends of popular websites. Writing C# is very similar to Java, although the language runs more similarly to C++. Additionally, Unity Game Engine uses C#, and it is the most popular way to make video games.
